Collecting Duck Eggs

How do I know if a duck egg is good?

Collecting duck eggs is easy. While it is safe to eat a fertalized egg, you probably want to get it before it is incubated (where the momma duck sits on it). I believe it's safe to say most people don't want to crack open an egg to find a half developed duck. If you are not sure if the egg has been incubated, get a flashlight and shine it through the egg. If the egg glows with bloodvesels, do not eat it. Check the table below to see how long your eggs will keep for.

Refridgerated Room Temp Incubated
3 months 6 weeks Do not consume
